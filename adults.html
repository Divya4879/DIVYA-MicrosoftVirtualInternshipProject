EXERCISE GUIDELINES

Well to be honest, you are in the stage where you will have mostly physically grown, have great metabolism , are at the best stage of your life, are overworked, overstressed. You have little time to spend on working out,have to focus on your peace of mind and mental well-being.
Well, I have these tips for you to incorporate a better lifestyle:-

  • 150-300 mins per week of sports/workout/moderate physical activity
    per week. (can be less if intense workout)
  • Strength training 2-4 times a week.
  • Lift heavy weights with compound movements.
  • Good posture
  • Plyometric exercises
  • Start with moderation
  • HIIT/TABATA/CIRCUIT TRAINING + STRETCHING
