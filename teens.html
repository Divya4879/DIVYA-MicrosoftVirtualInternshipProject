EXERCISE GUIDELINES

Well to be honest, you are in the stage where you will have your maximum growth, both physically and mentally. I would recommend you to be just active as much as possible throughout the day, I won't recommend gymming at this stage . I would suggest just playing sports, having plenty of sunlight, and cardio. I will also suggest some meditation for concentration power.
If you still want some basic guidelines , i would mention these:-

  • At least 300 mins per week of sports/workout/moderate physical activity per week.
  • Pranayama , meditation for lungs, peace and meditation.
  • Sports
  • Hiit workouts, cardio exercises, circuit training
  • Plyometric exercises
  • Start with moderation
  • Don't overstress yourself.
